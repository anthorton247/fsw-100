Express Yourself

The main focus of poetry is getting your thoughts and emotions understood. Don't feel down if you don't have the perfect rhyming pattern with match syllables. First in foremost your goal should be to convey YOUR message. What are you feeling. What does it compare to. Whether it a situation, place, an objection etc. Once you get your concept decided and drafted you can tune and tweak to be more harmonic.
